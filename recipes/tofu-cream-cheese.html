Tofu Cream Cheese


A thick and tangy homemade spread that's perfect for toast and bagels, in recipes or as a dip

This creamy vegan cream cheese is easy to make and requires only 7 simple ingredients. The base is delicious alone, but you can experiment and customize how you like! Mix in scallions, shredded carrots, and fresh herbs for a savory vegan spread. Alternatively, sweeten it up by incorporating raisins, cinnamon, and walnuts.

You will need:

  • 14 oz block Firm Tofu
  • 1 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 large Garlic Cloves
  • 3 tbsp Vegan Butter melted
  • 0.5-1 tsp Sea Salt

Instructions

  1. Drain the water from the package of tofuâ€” don't press it.
  2. Add the firm tofu to a blender or food processor along with the remaining ingredients.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy. A food processor may not blend as smooth as a blender; it might be a little grainy but it will still taste great.
  4. Pour into a container or bowl and chill in the fridge for 30 minutes to an hour before serving, if possible. The butter will harden and the texture will become smooth and creamy.
  5. Add any additional flavors and serve as desired! Enjoy!
